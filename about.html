Our Story

Where Beauty Meets Craftsmanship

Lathasri Makeovers was born from a passion for making women look and feel their absolute best. Founded with a simple belief — that every woman deserves to be beautiful — we started as a small beauty parlour and have grown into a full-service beauty and tailoring destination.

Over the years, we have helped hundreds of brides, working women, students, and mothers look stunning for their most important moments. From simple threading to elaborate bridal makeovers, and from everyday blouse stitching to intricate designer work — we pour our heart into every service.

Our Mission & Vision

What drives us every single day

Our Mission

To provide every client with professional-grade beauty and tailoring services in a warm, welcoming environment — at prices that are accessible to everyone in our community. We believe looking good should not be a luxury.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted beauty parlour and tailoring shop in our region — known not just for our craft, but for the confidence we give our clients. We aim to expand our services and reach more women who deserve to feel beautiful.

Our Core Values

The principles we work by every day

We use only high-quality, branded products and maintain strict hygiene standards. All tools are sterilized after every use.

Every client is unique. We listen carefully to your needs and customize every service to suit your face, skin type, and personal style.

No hidden charges. Our prices are clearly listed and we discuss all costs before starting any service. What you see is what you pay.

We respect your time. Appointments are honored, deliveries are on time, and we never compromise on our commitments.
